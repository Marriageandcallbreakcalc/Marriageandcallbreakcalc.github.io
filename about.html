About the Developer

Hi! I'm a solo developer who built the Marriage and Call Break Point Calculator app for iOS and Android.

I created this app because I love playing these traditional card games with friends and family, and wanted to make score keeping easier and more accurate.

My goal was to design an app that preserves the cultural heritage of these traditional South Asian card games while making them more accessible to everyone through modern technology.

About the App

Designed with card game enthusiasts in mind

Marriage & Call Break Calc is designed to be a simple yet powerful calculator for two popular card games. The app helps players track scores, validate moves, and enjoy their game without worrying about complex point calculations.

User-Friendly Interface

Clean, intuitive design that makes scorekeeping simple for players of all ages and tech-comfort levels.

Accurate Calculations

Complex game scoring rules are handled automatically, ensuring error-free results every time.

Game History

Track scores across multiple games and sessions to monitor performance over time.

Customizable Rules

Adjust scoring settings to match your group's specific house rules and preferences.

Cultural Preservation

Helping traditional South Asian card games thrive in the digital age with modern tools.
